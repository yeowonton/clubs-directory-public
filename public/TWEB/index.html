Troy Web Dev Club

Learn & develop

  • Why you should join

    • You will know the tools to put yourself out on the internet!
    • Can be used for college resumes and job opportunities
    • Build upon Computer Science skills!
    • Learn new programming languages!
    • Build up your digital portfolio!

  • Purpose and Goals

    Anyone can join and learn the basics or advanced techniques of how to design, construct, and use websites to their fullest potential! We will show freshman or anyone new to Computer Science the ropes and give them a head start in APCSP, or give them assistance in their Troy Tech Computer Science Pathway!

    We will:

    • Train you with the tools and knowledge of how to produce useful websites.
    • Help you learn how to collaborate with others.
    • Help you with creating and setting up a digital portfolio.
    • Offer you opportunities for competitions.

  • General Information

    • Access to competitions!
    • Promising career to pursue!
    • Club officers alternate every semester, so you can get club leadership easier!
    • Freshmen can learn basics of Computer Science!
